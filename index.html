I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate at the University of Notre Dame, under the supervision of Andy Putman.

Research Interests

I work in the intersection of topology and number theory. Specifically, I study Steinberg modules, and their applications to the cohomology of arithmetic groups. Along the way, I have used tools from geometric group theory, algebraic K-theory, and representation theory.

Papers

A Solomon-Tits theorem for rings.
